Check your daily money with IFTTT

Example:

@me: #ihave 10000
@ifttt: Wait a minutes
@ifttt: 588 a day

I hate financial planning but I want my daily spending to be more predictable.

At any time I know the amount of my free money and the number of days remaining until a wage. This information is enough to roughly calculate how much money I can spend at a day.

I want to use a familiar tool for this simple accounting of finance, so I wrote a small application that integrates with the IFTTT service which can interact with a lot of applications and services, for example Telegram.

Step-by-step:

  1. Log in at IFTTT service
  2. Find your unique key on the settings page.

    Replace KEY with key from the second step and DAY with your monthly pay day
